Lorenzo Dall'Amico

Postdoctoral fellow

ISI foundation

I am currently a postodoctoral fellowin the group led by Prof. Ciro Cattuto at the ISI foundation in Turin, Italy and contract professor at University of Torino.

I obtained my PhD working in the GAIA team of GIPSA lab in Grenoble, under the supervision of Prof. Romain Couillet and of Nicolas Tremblay. My work focused on the improvement and theoretical understanding of unsupervised graph spectral clustering with methods at the frontier between graph signal processing, statistical physics, computer science and random matrix theory. Check here the page dedicated to my PhD manuscript.

Earlier I got a master degree in Physics of complex systems at Politecnico di Torino and Université Paris Sud. Check here the page dedicated to my Master degree thesis.
